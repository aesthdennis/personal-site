Book Recommendations

    Military and Leadership

    I once asked myself: Which books would I require a grasp of in order to lead a group, nation or empire? The following books are to be used in order to enrich the philosophy required, with which one would tackle commanding with; it seeks to establish the bigger picture at its most fundamental. The bigger picture should be intuited, analysed and thought in clear depth, whereas details are to be embraced from experience and action alone. Once you have a vision established, it shall magnet details by itself, with no further need of examination.

    A complete individual—that has his home in order, goals in mind, shadow in check, empathy, sympathy and a personal vision in heart—is a competent leader, of which his men can rest their heads on.
