That Which Philosophizes

B. Denis

Written out of intuitions and spiritual reflections to provide myself with comfort for my neurotic fall within the abyss. A collection of notes comprised of philosophical musings, autobiographical analysis and observations upon metaphysical structures.

I've always struggled to understand myself and make sense of the world around me. As both a child and an adult, I've had one consistent question throughout my life; as to why am I a man, and not a bird--or a different man? How hard ought I had sinned, to be born and given life as such man?
